Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 March 2026

Who we are

Medicine Transparency is an informational website operated from Dublin, Ireland. For any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@medicinetransparency.eu.

What data we collect

We do not collect personal data through this website. There are no contact forms, user accounts, or sign-up features. We do not use cookies of any kind.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics tool, to understand how visitors use this site. Plausible does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track individual visitors across websites. All data is aggregated and anonymous.

Our Plausible instance is self-hosted on our own infrastructure within the EU. No analytics data is shared with third parties.

Third-party services

This website may load fonts or stylesheets from external content delivery networks. These services may receive your IP address as part of the standard HTTP request. No other third-party services are used.

Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we do not collect personal data through this website, there is typically no data to request.

If you have any concerns, contact us at hello@medicinetransparency.eu.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
